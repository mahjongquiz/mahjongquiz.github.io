If you have an open hand, and all of your pieces are in a combination, can you win?
Or do you also need to complete a winning method?

You always need to complete a winning method when your hand is open, to be able to win. Aswell as having all your pieces in combinations (4 groups of 3 and a pair).

There are over 20 winning methods and you only need to choose one of them to win.
